# Acme Robotics Employee Handbook (Synthetic)

## Paid Time Off

Full-time employees accrue 20 working days of paid time off (PTO) per
calendar year. PTO accrues monthly at a rate of 1.67 days per completed
month of service. Unused PTO carries over up to a cap of 30 days. Upon
termination, unused PTO up to the cap is paid out at the employee's
base rate.

Requests should be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the
HR portal. Approval is contingent on team coverage.

## Remote Work

Acme supports hybrid and fully remote arrangements for eligible roles.
Fully remote employees are expected to:

- Maintain core working hours of 10:00 to 15:00 in their assigned
  working timezone
- Attend quarterly in-person offsites at the San Jose headquarters
- Keep a dedicated, distraction-free workspace

Internet reimbursement is capped at USD 80 per month, claimed via the
expense system. Home office furniture is not reimbursed.

## Expenses

All work-related expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the
expense date. Receipts are required for any item above USD 25. Expense
categories include:

- Travel (flights, ground transport, lodging)
- Meals during business travel (per diem of USD 75 per day)
- Software and cloud services pre-approved by the employee's manager
- Conference attendance and continuing education

Personal purchases, alcohol over USD 30, and entertainment are not
reimbursable.

## Equipment

New hires receive a laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Standard
laptop is a MacBook Pro 14 inch or a ThinkPad X1 Carbon, selectable at
onboarding. Equipment replacement cycle is 36 months. Damaged or lost
equipment must be reported to IT within three business days.

## Termination

Voluntary termination requires two weeks written notice to the
employee's manager and HR. The final paycheck, including any accrued
PTO payout, is issued on the standard next payroll date. Company
equipment must be returned within seven calendar days of the last
working day.
