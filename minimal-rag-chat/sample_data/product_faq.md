# Widgetly Cloud FAQ (Synthetic)

## Pricing

Widgetly Cloud has three plans:

- **Starter** at USD 19 per month. Includes 5 projects, 10 GB storage,
  and community support.
- **Team** at USD 79 per month. Includes 50 projects, 100 GB storage,
  SSO, and email support with a 24 hour response SLA.
- **Enterprise**, custom pricing. Includes unlimited projects, custom
  storage, SAML SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated customer success
  manager.

All plans are billed monthly or annually. Annual billing is discounted
by 15 percent.

## Trial

New accounts receive a 14 day free trial of the Team plan. No credit
card is required. At the end of the trial, accounts are automatically
downgraded to the Starter plan unless an upgrade is selected.

## Usage Limits

Storage is measured in base-10 gigabytes (1 GB = 10^9 bytes). Soft
limits trigger an email warning at 80 percent utilization. Hard limits
block new uploads at 100 percent but never delete existing data.

API rate limits:

- Starter: 60 requests per minute per project
- Team: 600 requests per minute per project
- Enterprise: negotiated per contract

## Billing

Invoices are issued on the first day of each billing cycle. Payment
methods accepted: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and ACH for
annual Enterprise contracts. Refunds for annual plans are prorated to
the nearest full month.

Failed payments trigger a 7 day grace period. Accounts unpaid after 30
days are suspended. Suspended accounts retain data for 90 days before
permanent deletion.

## Cancellation

Plans can be cancelled at any time from the billing page. Cancellations
take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No prorated
refunds are issued for monthly plans.
